We build to live

We build to reconstruct the soul of our craft. A soul that's been reduced to dust, glued back together and placed on a shelf next to other mementos of foregone American to craftsmen eliminated by corporate demand. We build to reincarnate materials that have earned a fate better than death. Wood salvaged from a barn that once shielded a family's livelihood. Metal with legions of scars to prove its unwavering strength and resilience - materials that stood for something. WE DON'T BUILD IN CHINA. We build where we live. We build in a community comprised of talented artisans who make more than just dollars by the hour. A brotherhood that challenges us to do better work & refuses to let us outsource our inspiration. Salt Lake City, Utah. We're here for a reason. We build in a space void of a front office, receptionist, or an HR department. A space that embodies our ideals and lets each of us stand behind our work. Because we don't oil our saws to build disposable, part-time bullshit. We build furniture we're proud of with warranties that can't be measured in lifetimes. FURNITURE BUILT FOREVER.
